Project Outline:
Measuring the centrality of nodes in graphs has become a research topic with many applications including analyzing social, internet, and transportation networks. Part of the research encompasses recomputing centrality for nodes after an update to the graph. For this project, I will be covering some graph theory but will mainly be focussing on recent parallel algorithms for updating centrality in dynamic graphs. I hope to implement the algorithm designed by Skula et al. on a multi-core CPU and test the algorithm’s performance on additional graph sets.
